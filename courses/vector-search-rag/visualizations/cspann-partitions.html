How C-SPANN Works

C-SPANN partitions vectors into clusters aligned with CockroachDB ranges. During search, only the nearest partitions are probed—drastically reducing scans at scale.

  • Partition centroids act as cluster representatives
  • Query finds nearest centroids first
  • Only vectors in nearest partitions are scanned
  • Rest of database skipped entirely
